## Side Quest Encounters (Travel Route)

### Ice Mage
*Location: The Ice Church (northern route)*

The Ice Mage is summoning Ice Trolls through a rift inside the church. The longer the rift remains open (i.e., the longer the Mage lives), the more Ice Trolls appear. Use the standard **Archmage** stat block (CR 12) or scale to the party level.

### Ice Trolls
*Location: The Ice Church (northern route)*

Use standard **Troll** stat block (CR 5) with cold-themed reskinning. They are immune to cold damage but retain the standard regeneration and fire/acid vulnerability.
