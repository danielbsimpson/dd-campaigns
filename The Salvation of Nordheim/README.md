# The Salvation of Nordheim

A D&D 5e campaign set in a Viking-inspired frozen north, spanning a journey from a quiet southern town to an epic confrontation in the icy mountains of Nordheim.

---

## Overview

A Viking warrior named **Bjorn Ironclad** arrives at the party's tavern seeking champions. The Kingdom of **Nordheim** is under siege — its villages fallen and people terrorized by a monstrous creature known as the **Hrimfang**. What begins as a rescue mission unravels into something far more sinister than anyone expected.

**Tone:** Norse mythology, mystery, survival horror, epic adventure  
**Setting:** A journey through coastal port towns, frozen tundra, and icy mountain lairs  
**Themes:** Deception, resilience, the nature of monsters, heroism

---

### Act 0 — The Recruitment

Bjorn Ironclad, a broad-shouldered Viking warrior in etched armor and a fur-lined cloak, enters the tavern with a purpose. He speaks of **King Ragnar Frostbeard** and the terror of the **Hrimfang** — a monstrous creature ravaging the Kingdom of Nordheim. Villages have fallen, hope is fading, and the king needs champions.

Bjorn has a ship waiting in the port town of **Hafnheim**, ready to sail north.

**Bjorn Ironclad:** Born under the Northern Lights, raised by a lineage of warriors. Loyal, scarred by battle, driven by duty. He serves as guide and companion through the early acts of the campaign.

---

### Act 1 — Hafnheim

A bustling coastal port town with colorful tents, a grand Mead Hall, and an undercurrent of trouble. Farmers are losing cattle, and a band of raiders has been terrorizing the trade routes.

#### Key Locations

| Location | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Gullveig's Hearth** (Mead Hall) | Reik Ironbeard | Heart of the town; tales of the dragon attacks and suspicious old castle to the north |
| **Skjold's Rest** (Inn) | Astrid Stormshield | Warm lodging; a sailor who survived a shipwreck; a trustworthy source of rumors |
| **Ironbeard's Forge** (Blacksmith) | Durg Ironbeard | High-quality weapons and armor, including a Dwarven Thrower |
| **Arcane Enclaves** (Enchanter) | Aeliana | Potions, scrolls, and alchemical items |
| **Bifrost Bazaar** (Market Square) | — | Ropes, torches, rations, and general supplies |
| **Temple of the Allfather** | — | Odin's temple; blessings and sacred relics before heading north |

#### Key NPCs

- **Reik Ironbeard** — Jovial mead hall owner; a former adventurer who witnessed the dragon; suspects the old castle to the north holds answers
- **Astrid Stormshield** — Inn owner; resilient sailor with keen insight; believes the castle is linked to both the dragon and the bandits
- **Durg Ironbeard** — Gruff master blacksmith; master craftsman

#### Side Quests

**1. Cattle Conundrum**
Local farmers are losing their cattle to a dragon terrorizing the fields. The party can track the creature to its lair by following signs of its attacks and gathering information from victims and the mead hall.
- *Boss:* Young White Dragon (CR 6)
- *Reward:* Frostband Dagger, Hat of Disguise, 1,500 gold / 400 silver / 5,000 copper

**2. The Blackshore Raiders**
A notorious bandit gang called the **Blackshore Raiders** has been ambushing caravans and travelers near Hafnheim. The party must investigate the attacks, track the raiders into the marshlands, infiltrate their fortified tower, and confront their leader.
- *Boss:* Grimm Blackthorn — Bandit Captain (CR 5)
- *Reward:* Dagger of Venom, Boots of the Winterlands, Captain's Chest (1,500 gold + 3 Black Pearls)

---

### Act 2 — Frostgate

A small, quiet coastal town on the northern continent — the first port of call in Nordheim. Modest but full of character. Two local mysteries draw curious adventurers.

#### Key Locations

| Location | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **The Frosted Mug** (Tavern) | Ingrid Oakheart | Warm hearth, hearty food, tales of local mysteries |
| **The Frostgate Rest** (Inn) | Sigurd Froststrider | Former adventurer; fascinated by the town's frozen mysteries; encourages the party to investigate |
| **Nordic Traders** (General Store) | Eirik Skaldhammer | Maps, winter gear, scrolls, and supplies |
| **Fireforge's Forge** (Blacksmith) | Brogar Ironforge | Repairs and mechanical work; limited stock due to the frozen lighthouse|
| **The Fisherman's Catch** (Market) | — | Fresh seafood from the harbor |

#### Side Quests

**1. The Frozen Lighthouse**
An ancient lighthouse off the coast has been dormant for centuries, its light extinguished. With the harbor suffering from the lack of safe guidance, an old fisherman named Olaf asks the party to restore it. Getting inside, repairing the mechanism, and relighting the beacon each present their own challenges.
- *Reward:* Scroll of Ice Storm, gemstones (Sapphires, Emeralds, Rubies), access to the lighthouse's magical beacon signal

**2. The Mysterious Iceberg**
An enormous iceberg appeared off the coast after a recent blizzard, and local scholars believe an ancient artifact — the **Frostheart Crystal** — lies sealed within a hidden chamber inside. A scholar named Elinor seeks the party's help deciphering its puzzle. The chamber is unstable and won't stay above water for long.
- *Challenge:* A multi-part elemental puzzle within a sinking chamber — time is running out once you enter
- *Reward:* Longbow of Warning

---

### Act 3 — Travel to Nordheim

The overland journey to the Kingdom of Nordheim is long and perilous. The wide frozen wilderness offers both danger and discovery.

#### Chance Encounters

Winter Wolves, Ice Goblins, Frost Giant Scouts, Winter Wights, Ice Mephits, Remorhaz, Dire Polar Bear, Frost Salamander

#### Side Quests Along the Route

**Route 1 — The Mysterious Statue in the Woods** *(southern forest path)*
Deep in the forest, the party discovers an ancient stone statue partially overgrown with vines. Inscriptions in two languages adorn its sides, and a third inscription appears at the front. Deciphering the meaning and acting on it at the right time may yield a reward.
- *Reward:* Ring of Protection

**Route 2 — The Ice Church** *(northern mountain path)*
An eerily beautiful church constructed entirely of ice stands in the frozen wilderness, surrounded by figures frozen in place. Inside, something sinister stirs — an Ice Mage has opened a rift and is summoning creatures through it. The longer the rift stays open, the worse it gets.
- *Enemies:* Ice Mage, Ice Trolls  
- *Reward:* Cape of Mountebank

**The Maze of Whispers** *(appears after several days of travel)*
A hooded figure named **Morvus** materializes at the forest's edge and beckons the party toward a mystical labyrinth called the Maze of Whispers. The maze can only be entered one at a time, and each player must work together to understand and navigate it fully.
- *Reward:* Boots of Elvenkind

---

### Act 4 — The Kingdom of Nordheim

A great Viking kingdom carved from stone and ice, now scarred by siege. Its once-bustling streets are quiet. The few souls still walking them carry the weight of years of fear.

#### Approaching the Gates

The city's walls and watchtowers bear the marks of prolonged battle. A towering statue of King Ragnar watches over the gates. The party is escorted to the Great Hall.

#### King Ragnar's Welcome

**King Ragnar Frostbeard** greets the party with a feast in the Great Hall. He recounts the history of the **Hrimfang** — once a distant creature haunting the wilderness — and the recent escalation: smaller creatures, striking inside the towns themselves, dragging people into the dark.

That very night, the hall is attacked. What the party discovers changes everything.

#### Post-Siege City

After the battle, Nordheim begins to breathe again. New merchants and settlers arrive, and the city's shops reopen.

| Location | Owner | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Aurora Anvil** (Blacksmith) | Ragnhild Ironhand | Enchanted armor with runic designs; frost-resistant equipment |
| **Shieldmaiden's Armory** (Weapons) | Astrid Ironheart | Battleaxes, greatswords, Viking weaponry |
| **The Mead Hall** (Tavern) | Sigrid Aleheart | Warm gathering place; tales of the siege and renewed hope |
| **Valkyrie's Apothecary** | Ingrid Windrider | Elixirs, tinctures, and healing potions; arcane and mysterious |
| **Raven's Rest Inn** | Eirik Ravenshadow | Lodging with a haunted, quiet atmosphere |
| **The Wise Raven's Bookshop** | Eirik Ravenshadow | Ancient tomes, spell codexes, traveler's guides |
| **Odin's Blessing Temple** | High Priestess Freya | Blessed talismans, sacred waters, prayer items |

#### Side Quest — The Haunting of Nordholm Abbey

Whispers spread of a chilling presence at Nordholm Abbey, an ancient monastery on the city's outskirts. Skeletal monks, restless specters, and mournful wails have driven the locals away. The source of the haunt is a powerful necromancer's spirit trapped between realms — it must be confronted and freed.
- *Boss:* Malevolent Necromancer (Ghost, CR 6) — with legendary actions and undead summons
- *Reward:* Cloak of Displacement, Scroll of Spirit Guardians, Scroll of Speak with Dead

---

### Act 5 — The Mountain Lair

Following the trail left after Nordheim's attack, the party tracks their quarry to the base of the great mountains. A massive cave entrance is guarded by warriors of the **Frostfang Clan** — a cannibal tribe that worships and emulates the very creature terrorizing Nordheim.

A hidden side entrance offers a stealthier approach through a maze of twisting tunnels, crude altars, and dark chambers.

#### The Frostfang Clan

These warriors paint themselves in icy blue tribal markings, wear furs crafted to mimic their god's appearance, and use shamans skilled in frost magic. As the party pushes deeper, the truth of what has been happening to Nordheim becomes clear.

#### Devourer's Den

At the heart of the mountain lair, the party meets the true power behind it all: the **Frostbane Matriarch**, an ancient and powerful ice elemental who commands the Frostclaw Yetis and directs the Frostfang Clan's raids. A formidable final boss — wielding devastating ice magic and capable of summoning reinforcements.

- *Boss:* Frostbane Matriarch — Ice Elemental (CR 12) with legendary resistance, blizzard summoning, and a frosty aura
- *Reward:* Cloak of Protection, Frostbite Blade *(see Key Rewards)*

---

### Act 6 — Valkyriehaven

With the Frostbane Matriarch defeated and the Kingdom saved, King Ragnar bestows the legendary **Frostbite Blade** upon the party and provides a royal escort to **Valkyriehaven** — a newly revitalized port town near Nordheim.

The town square holds a freshly erected statue of the party, and the King has arranged three nights at the **Valkyrie's Rest** inn and a guaranteed passage home whenever the party is ready to sail.

#### The Ending

As the party celebrates in the Valkyrie's Rest, the tavern door bursts open — a hooded figure enters, no taller than four feet, moving with urgent purpose toward the party.

A new adventure awaits.
