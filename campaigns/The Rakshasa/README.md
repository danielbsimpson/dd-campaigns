# The Rakshasa

A D&D 5e campaign set in a South Asian-inspired kingdom, designed for parties of levels 12¢14. A shapeshifting demon has spent decades hollowing out a prosperous city from within Ś and the adventurers are the only ones who can stop it.

> For full scene descriptions, encounter details, and DM notes, see [The Rakshasa.txt](The%20Rakshasa.txt).

---

## Overview

An exhausted envoy arrives seeking outside help. The great city of **Vethara** is dying from within Ś nobles behave strangely, trusted allies betray their friends, and the ruling council grows more erratic with each passing moon. Behind it all, **Vikramasura** Ś an ancient Rakshasa of terrible cunning Ś has quietly replaced Vethara's most powerful figures one by one, building toward a ritual that will cement its dominion over the region forever.

**Tone:** Political intrigue, social deception, investigation, mounting horror
**Setting:** The walled city of Vethara and the surrounding jungle
**Themes:** Identity and trust, illusion versus reality, corrupting power, divine intervention

---

## The City of Vethara

Vethara is a wealthy, walled city-state built along the banks of the sacred **Mala River**, famed for its spice trade, towering temples, and the richest bazaar in the known world. From the outside it appears prosperous and magnificent Ś but merchants vanish, priests are found dead at their altars, and those who have known Vethara's leaders for decades whisper that they no longer recognize them.

## Campaign Structure

### Act 0 Ś The Call
An exhausted envoy delivers a sealed letter from Captain Priya Deva urging the party to come to Vethara quietly, as ordinary travelers. Something inside the city is wearing the faces of its leaders Ś and it must not know it is being hunted.

---

### Act 1 Ś The City of Vethara
The party arrives to a city that looks prosperous but feels wrong. After meeting Priya Deva in secret at the Crimson Lotus Inn, they are given a dossier on four active situations that, if investigated, will reveal the shape of the conspiracy. Much of this act is social and investigative.

#### Side Quests

| Quest | Summary | Enemies | Reward |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Merchant's Thread** | Find a missing spice merchant; trail leads to a Cambion-run smuggling ring moving ritual components for the Rakshasa | Cambion, Shadow Demons, Bearded Devils | 500gp + *Necklace of Fireballs*; Siva recruited as informant |
| **The Temple of Indra** | Three priests murdered and sacred texts stolen; a Night Hag coven is harvesting souls for the preparation ritual | Night Hag ū 3, Shadow Demons | *Death Ward* blessing + *Eye of the Hag*; codex pages reveal the ritual's final phase |
| **The Missing Children** | Eleven children abducted from the Sunken Ward by an Oni posing as a welfare inspector; Chain Devils guard the underground prison | Oni, Chain Devil ū 2 | Sunken Ward intelligence network + *Amulet of the Devout* |
| **The False General** | General Abhaya has been replaced by a Nycaloth; exposing it prevents a staged assassination at the upcoming coronation ceremony | Nycaloth, Bearded Devil ū 4 | General's military ring + *Ring of Mind Shielding* |

---

### Act 2 Ś The Shadow Network
Evidence from the side quests converges: every thread connects to a sealed wing of the Palace and the Grand Vizier's supply network. Working through Siva and the Night Bazaar, the party traces the operation to a fortified underground vault guarded by Bone Devils Ś inside are ledgers and Infernal communications proving Vikramasura's identity.

- *Enemies:* Bone Devil ū 2, Cult Fanatics

---

### Act 3 Ś The Palace of Whispers
The party must get their evidence in front of Maharaja Vardhan while Vikramasura controls every formal channel of access to the throne. When they finally force an audience and the consecrated incense (provided by Yogi Ananda) reveals the Vizier's true form, Vikramasura abandons its disguise, taunts the party, and tears through the palace wall into the jungle Ś with the ritual nearly complete.

- *Boss Encounter (escape fight):* Vikramasura (Rakshasa, CR 13), Erinyes ū 2

---

### Act 4 Ś The Jungle Road
The party pursues Vikramasura through guarded jungle approaches to its hidden sanctum. Four encounter zones protect the approaches, including a Nycaloth patrol, Shadow Demon ambushes, and a Yuan-ti outpost. At the temple gate, a **Guardian Naga** Ś bound against its will for decades Ś will stand aside and share temple intelligence in exchange for a promise of freedom after the battle.

- *Enemies:* Chain Devil, Shadow Demon ū 2, Oni, Yuan-ti Abomination
- *Conditional Ally:* Guardian Naga (CR 10)

---

### Act 5 Ś The Temple of Vikramasura
Three ascending tiers lead to the inner sanctum. Destroying ritual circles in Tier 2 reduces Vikramasura's maximum HP in the final fight (-10 HP per circle; 6+ circles destroyed means the Efreeti enters Phase 2 at half HP). The final confrontation is a two-phase boss fight Ś Vikramasura alone first, then Vikramasura plus a fully summoned Efreeti.

| Tier | Name | Key Threat |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hall of False Faces | Shadow Demons, Cambions; disorienting mirror maze |
| 2 | Ritual Chambers | Bone Devil ū 2, Erinyes; destroy circles to weaken the final boss |
| 3 | Inner Sanctum | Vikramasura (CR 13) + Efreeti (CR 11) Ś two-phase boss fight |

---

### Act 6 Ś Resolution
With Vikramasura destroyed, the remaining fiends scattered across Vethara unravel and are rooted out by Priya and the restored General Abhaya. Maharaja Vardhan hosts a grand ceremony honoring the party. Before the celebration, Yogi Ananda pulls the party aside with a quiet warning: Vikramasura belonged to a lineage, and others of that lineage will have noticed what happened here.
