# D&D Campaigns

A collection of homebrew D&D 5e campaign booklets. Each campaign is self-contained with a full narrative structure, NPC roster, location guide, and creature reference with D&D Beyond links.

---

## Campaigns

### [The Curse of the Pharaoh](The%20Curse%20of%20the%20Pharaoh/README.md)
**Setting:** Egyptian-inspired desert world — the ancient city of Saqaar  
**Tone:** Ancient mystery, desert survival, puzzle-solving, epic mythology  
**Themes:** Resurrection, prophecy, time, divine power  

The adventurers are recruited by three foreign merchants to save the great city of Saqaar. What begins as a hunt for a stolen artefact escalates into a race to prevent the return of the god Anubis and the end of an age.

### [The Salvation of Nordheim](The%20Salvation%20of%20Nordheim/README.md)
**Setting:** Viking-inspired frozen north — tundra, port towns, and icy mountain lairs  
**Tone:** Norse mythology, mystery, survival horror, epic adventure  
**Themes:** Deception, resilience, the nature of monsters, heroism  

A Viking warrior arrives seeking champions. The Kingdom of Nordheim is under siege by a monstrous creature known as the Hrimfang. What begins as a rescue mission unravels into something far more sinister than anyone expected.

### [The Rakshasa](The%20Rakshasa/README.md)
**Setting:** South Asian-inspired kingdom — the walled city of Vethara and surrounding jungle  
**Party Level:** 12–14  
**Tone:** Political intrigue, urban investigation, culminating in a dungeon assault  
**Themes:** Illusion, corruption, identity, the price of power  

A shapeshifting demon has spent decades hollowing out a prosperous city from within, replacing key figures with loyal duplicates. The adventurers are the only ones who can stop it — but they must first figure out who is real.

## Creature Reference Format

Each `creatures.md` file contains:
- A **Quick Reference table** listing all creatures by CR, type, and location
- Per-creature entries organised by act/location, including:
  - D&D Beyond link (numeric ID format where confirmed)
  - Condensed stat block (AC, HP, Speed, key traits, actions)
  - Campaign-specific role notes and encounter tips

Paywalled Monster Manual entries are noted with `*` and require a D&D Beyond Monster Manual licence to view the full stat block.
